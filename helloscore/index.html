Frequently Asked Questions
Does checking my score affect it?
No. HelloScore uses a soft inquiry which does not affect your credit score. Only hard inquiries from lenders impact your score.
How often is my score updated?
Your Experian score is refreshed every 30 days. You'll get a push notification as soon as your new score is ready.
How do I dispute an error on my report?
Go to Report, tap on the incorrect account, then tap Raise Dispute. We'll guide you through the process with Experian.
What is included in Premium?
Premium includes full credit report, DPD history, hard inquiry details, score alerts, dispute assistance, and priority customer support.
